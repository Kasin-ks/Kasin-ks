## Immigration Pathway Overview

1. **2-year Master degree** qualifies for **485 Post-Study Work Visa** (2-3 years work rights after graduation)
2. During 485 visa, gain work experience and improve English scores
3. Apply for PR via:
   - **189 (Skilled Independent)** - no state sponsorship needed
   - **190 (State Nominated)** - requires state nomination
   - **491 (Skilled Work Regional)** - regional area requirement
4. Occupation must be on Skilled Occupation List + pass skills assessment by relevant authority
